WELCOME!

The AUBAdvisor application was created to help students at AUB keep track of, and register for the correct courses during their stay at the university. The motivation was to ensure that students do not waste time and money taking courses that mistakingly don't count towards their degree, and to ensure that all students graduate on their intended time. The mobile and desktop compatible application is personal as each student can create an account and keep track of their progress every semester. To ensure that courses count towards their degree, the application displays their remaining major and General Education required courses. If a student attempts to take a course that isn't counted towards their degree, they will be notified.

AUBAdvisor also provides general information for all updates regarding courses. It can be updated dynamically via an administrator with access to the database. The application will allow for students to receive the latest news of changes in their faculty in the fastest way possible. This will resolve the conflict that results from a lack of communication between the the administration and the advisors, the advisors and the students, and the annual printing of the course catalog which contains contents that are often outdated.

We hope you find it useful!

Sincerly, The AUBAdvisor Team
